The society was registered as a transport SACCO in 1992 with a total membership of 40 drawn from the then disbanded Matatu Owners Association. Currently, the membership stands at 500 members.

Our members are drawn from three main electoral zones.

NYERI

Covering Embu, Kerugoya, Kangema, Thika, Karatina, Kutus, Nairobi and Mwea.

NYAHURURU

Covering Maralal, Rumuruti, Nanyuki, Isiolo and Olkalou.

NAKURU

Covering Naivasha, Eldoret, Kericho and Kisumu.

OUR VISION

To create and maintain an efficient nationally competitive strong transport SACCO, professionally managed and empowering all its stakeholders.

OUR MISSION

To mobilize savings and provide credit, offer efficient transport and other diversified services, through prudent management, motivating staff for optimum returns to our members and other stakeholders.
